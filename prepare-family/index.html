Prepare your Family

Is your family prepared?

Being prepared for bushfires beforehand is essential in guaranteeing the safety of you and your family. Here you will find some tips and checklists to ensure you and your loved ones are ready before a bushfire strikes.

Ring your Council

To enquire if the area you live in is bushfire prone.

Learn First Aid

To ensure care can be administered as soon as necessary in case of an emergency.

Know how to operate Utilities

To ensure water, gas and electricity supplies are switched off during evacuation.

TEACH YOUR CHILDREN

To call Triple Zero (000) in the event of a bushfire emergency.

You should store the kit in a place that is:
     ✓ safe
     ✓ easy to access, and
     ✓ known to everyone in your family so you can Grab and Go!

ADDITIONAL TIPS

Maintain an Emergency Contacts list

  • This list should include (at minimum):
  • Triple Zero (000)
  • Your family members
  • Your personal physician(s)

Maintain a Nearby Health Facilities list

  • This list should include (at minimum):
  • Hospitals
  • Clinics
  • Pharmacies
  • Terms & Conditions
  • Privacy Policy
