Aware yourself with this emergency sign

During an emergency people can respond in different ways. Some may stand still and freeze in their place.

Fire Safety Signs can help greatly during an emergency because the signs will not only help occupants to know where to go or what to do – the signs can also help leaders and fire marshals to locate the right equipment to fight the fir

FIRE BLANKETS

Fire blankets can be used to extinguish small fires in the home, caravan, boat or garage. They can also be used to wrap around a person whose clothes have caught alight.

 

 

 

 

FIRE CALL POINTS

These signs are installed to instantly show occupants of a commercial or publically accessed building the location of fire equipment, such as fire alarms, fire extinguishers, fire hoses and emergency stop buttons.

DO NOT USE LIFT

FIRE EXIT

Fire exit signs are used to clearly inform occupants of a building of the nearest emergency exits, safe evacuation route and general evacuation procedures. They can also offer detailed instructions, such as how to open doorways.

 

 

 

EMERGENCY TELEPHONE NUMBER

An Emergency Telephone Sign uses safety messages and symbols for emergency response protocol and compliance. An Emergency Telephone Sign identifies the location of an emergency telephone system in case of a fire emergency

 

 

 

 

 

FIRST AID

Emergency Sign ( Mobility impairment )

EMERGENCY EVACUATION CHAIR

This is a proposed sign for refuge areas and other areas that have Emergency Evacuation Chairs. It incorporates the use of the Accessible Means of Egress Icon Design and represents a person being evacuated using an evacuation chair, whilst another person using a wheelchair is waiting in a Refuge Area (i.e. the enlarged fire stair landing).
