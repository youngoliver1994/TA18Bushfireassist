Protect Your Livestock

Are your livestock safe?

Protecting your livestock is just as important as ensuring your household is safe in the event of a bushfire. Here you’ll find a few tips you can follow to ensure the safety of your livestock.

DESIGNATE A grazing area

Where your livestock can be evacuated to in the event of a bushfire.

MAKE FENCES STOCK-PROOF

To prevent your livestock from going astray during a bushfire.

LOCATE NEARBY WATER SOURCE

So you can ensure access to it upon evacuation for your livestock to drink from.

PREPARE ENOUGH FOOD

For the sustenance and nourishment of your livestock upon evacuation.

MORE TIPS:

What should I consider when looking for a grazing area?

You should ensure that:

  • It is easily accessible.
  • It is not situated in a bushy area.
  • The space is sufficient for your livestock to graze.
Who should I call in the event I require livestock assistance?

Please call Agriculture Victoria on 1800 226 226
