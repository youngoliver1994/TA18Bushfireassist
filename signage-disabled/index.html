During an emergency people can respond in different ways. Some may stand still and freeze in their place.

Fire Safety Signs can help greatly during an emergency because the signs will not only help occupants to know where to go or what to do – the signs can also help leaders and fire marshals to locate the right equipment to fight the fir

This is a proposed sign for refuge areas and other areas that have Emergency Evacuation Chairs. It incorporates the use of the Accessible Means of Egress Icon Design and represents a person being evacuated using an evacuation chair, whilst another person using a wheelchair is waiting in a Refuge Area (i.e. the enlarged fire stair landing).
