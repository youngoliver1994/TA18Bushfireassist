Medical Centres in your Region

It is essential to be well-aware of general/primary care medical centres within your region to ensure medical assistance can be received when required.
To find your nearest primary care medical centre:
  • Enter a Regional Government Area or Postcode in the search box below
  • Select Display Distances if you permit location detection to estimate distances from medical centres (please skip if otherwise)
  • Click on the map icon in the search results to be redirected to Google Maps to find directions to your chosen medical centre
